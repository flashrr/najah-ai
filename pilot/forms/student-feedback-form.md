# Student Feedback Form — Najah AI Pilot
# Ready to copy into Google Forms

## Setup instructions
1. Go to forms.google.com → Blank form
2. Title: "Najah AI — Ton avis compte ! 📚"
3. Description: "Ce formulaire prend 5 minutes. Tes réponses sont anonymes et aident à améliorer l'application pour toi et tes camarades."
4. Copy each question below exactly. Match the question type noted.
5. Make all questions optional EXCEPT Q1 (overall rating) and Q8 (biggest problem).
6. Share link via WhatsApp on Day 10.
